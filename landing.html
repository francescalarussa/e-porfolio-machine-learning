Team Project

Evidence of my contribution to the Unit 6 team project, including project outcomes, teamwork activities and evaluation.

Project Overview

The aim of the project was to develop a machine learning solution to identify pricing patterns and listing segments to Airbnb hosts in setting competitive prices.
Using the AB_NYC_2019 dataset, the team developed a regression model for price prediction and applied K-Means clustering to identify groups of listings with similar characteristics.
